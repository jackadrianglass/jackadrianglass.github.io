## So... why make a blog?

Well the real answer is "because I wanted to", but that seems insufficient. See a couple of months before the writing of this blog post, I got let go from my job (a disagreement on policy, nothing dramatic though very shocking for me). This left me unemployed with a lot of time on my hands.

I thought that it would be a good idea to make a website to have an online presence. I'm not one for social media. But as I was putting together this website, the thought of writing a blog seemed more and more appealing.

At my previous jobs, I found it very satisfying to take a bunch of information, pull it together, and explain it concisely. I made confluence pages about how C++ build times get really bad, I wrote about overuse of interfaces in object oriented programming, and I really loved doing it. I loved researching, reading, and putting this information together. It was to the point that at one job, I led the team for monthly lunch and learns.

I also love reading blogs. Some of my favorite sources are
- [This Week in Rust](https://this-week-in-rust.org/) which curates blogs about Rust
- [Amos' blog](https://fasterthanli.me/) which is all about their projects and work in Rust
- [nushell's blog](https://www.nushell.sh/blog/) which honestly is mostly release notes, but I love reading them

And there's more that I'm not listing. They're not flashy, they don't demand my attention, and loads of them helped me figure out how to do things for work. They're quietly awesome. I want to participate in that. So, here's what I'm planning on writing about.

## Topics

I'm a software developer by trade, but I don't just want to write about that. I have a lot of hobbies and it seems to me that there's lessons that could be transferred between them. So, I want to write about
- _Software Side Projects_ - I'm thinking of these as little playgrounds for me to learn
- _Guitar/Drums/Music_ - Updates on how I'm playing, what I'm listening to, and how I'm going about learning to write music
- _Curated Stuff_ - I like curated lists. I'm gonna put together my own and describe what I like about them
- _Thoughts/Reactions to Content_ - Rather than brainlessly consume the internet, I want to pause and think

With all this unexpected time on my hands, I finally have space and opportunity to do something new. I'm glad that you're here with me and I'll see you next time.
